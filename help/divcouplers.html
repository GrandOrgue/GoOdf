Divisional Couplers

A divisional coupler is a drawstop object. If enabled, activating a divisional on one controlled manual will activate the corresponding divisional on referenced manuals. It can be controlled by switches and its is configured for the most part like Stops, ranks, couplers, and tremulants. Additionally, it has the following unique parameters:

Is bidirectional. (Boolean, required) If false, the coupler only couples upward in the referenced manual list of the coupler, else upward and downward.
